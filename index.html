About

The person behind the data

I'm a data and operations professional who has worked at the intersection of large-scale field operations, supply chain logistics, and political analytics. My career has been defined by one thing: building systems that work for people operating in the field — not just for analysts sitting in offices.

At I-PAC, I ran data operations for campaigns that touched every corner of Bihar — tracking beneficiary outreach for 3 crore families, mapping 50,000 villages, and managing ground teams of 100+ field personnel. At Crystal Cold Chain, I built the full suite of internal tools from scratch — a Yard Command Center, Finance Portal, and Service Complaint system used daily by technicians, managers, and accounts teams.

My approach is end-to-end: I design the data architecture, build the pipeline, create the dashboard, and train the people using it. Operations intelligence isn't just about the data — it's about making the data move the right people to do the right things.
